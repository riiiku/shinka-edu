SGU English Programs

The Super Global University (SGU) project, initiated by Japan's Ministry of Education, encompasses English-taught programs at 37 of Japan's top universities, including the University of Tokyo, Waseda, and Keio. No Japanese language skills are required — apply and study entirely in English.

Ideal For

  • Strong English proficiency (TOEFL 90+ / IELTS 6.5+)
  • International education background or exchange experience
  • Those who want to study in Japan without learning Japanese
  • Efficiency-oriented applicants seeking a streamlined path

What We Provide

  • SGU program selection and strategy planning
  • PS / SoP / Research proposal guidance and editing
  • Recommendation letter strategy and coordination
  • Mock interview preparation
  • Full progress management and document review

Research Student (Kenkyusei)

The "kenkyusei" system is a pre-master's research period unique to Japanese graduate schools. By contacting professors directly (a process known as "sōji" or professor outreach) and securing informal acceptance ("nainei"), this is a primary pathway into Japan's top research labs.

Ideal For

  • Clear research direction and academic interests
  • Japanese N2+ or strong English ability
  • Planning long-term academic development in Japan
  • Prefer to adapt as a research student before the master's exam

What We Provide

  • Research direction planning and professor matching
  • Professor outreach email drafting and strategy
  • Research proposal guidance (800-1200 chars in Japanese)
  • Professor meeting preparation and mock sessions
  • Formal application materials and submission

Direct Examination (Chokuju)

Take Japan's graduate school entrance exams (written test + interview) head-on and earn formal master's admission on your own merits. This is the most "hardcore" application route and the best way to demonstrate academic strength.

Ideal For

  • JLPT N1 or near-N1 Japanese proficiency
  • Currently in a Japanese language school or undergraduate program in Japan
  • Strong academic foundation with confidence in written exams
  • Want to skip the kenkyusei phase and enter master's directly

What We Provide

  • Target school and department selection
  • Exam subject analysis and study guidance
  • Research proposal and statement of purpose writing
  • Interview preparation and mock practice
  • Timeline planning and document preparation
