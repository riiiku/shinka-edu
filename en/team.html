A Message from Our Founder

The reason I started Xinjia Education is simple: I wanted every student aspiring to study in Japan to receive truly professional, sincere help — not to be treated as just another "signed contract."

In this industry, we've seen too much anxiety caused by information asymmetry. Students who chose the wrong agency and missed the school that was perfect for them. Students whose research proposals weren't good enough and lost their chance with their target professor. I don't want these regrets to keep happening.

That's why we insist on boutique service — our team isn't large, but every member is deeply expert in their area. We don't make false promises or over-package. We simply use our real capabilities and genuine care to help you navigate this journey well.

—— Jaden · Founder, Xinjia Education
